Preparation for BÃ©chamel Sauce

Melt the butter

Put the butter in a saucepan and melt over low heat.

Add the flour

After the butter is completely melted, add the flour and roast for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly. You should stir until the flour loses its light smell, but do not let it darken in color.

Add the milk

While adding the milk little by little, mix it quickly with a whisk. This will prevent lumps.

Make sure it thickens

Continue cooking over medium heat, stirring constantly. When the sauce starts to thicken, add salt and optional black pepper and grated nutmeg.
