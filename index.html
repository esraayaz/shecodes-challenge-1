üë®‚Äçüç≥ Preparation for B√©chamel Sauce:

  1. Melt the butter: Put the butter in a saucepan and melt over low heat.
  2. Add the flour: After the butter is completely melted, add the flour and roast for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly. You should stir until the flour loses its light smell, but do not let it darken in color.
  3. Add the milk: While adding the milk little by little, mix it quickly with a whisk. This will prevent lumps.
  4. Make sure it thickens: Continue cooking over medium heat, stirring constantly. When the sauce starts to thicken, add salt and optional black pepper and grated nutmeg.
  5. Consistency control: The consistency of the sauce should be runny but thick. If it is too thick, you can add a little more milk. Bon Appetit! üòä
